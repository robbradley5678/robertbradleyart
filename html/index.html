Hello

I am a self taught landscape painter from Manchester, UK. I work mainly in acrylic and watercolour, capturing scenes from the natural world - open grasslands, rolling hills, proud trees and big skies, with the occasional still life thrown in.

Painting has opened my eyes to the beauty of nature, something that's too easy to take for granted. Connecting with nature makes us feel happy, uplifted and calm. My aim is to capture these feelings on canvas, and bring a little bit of the great outdoors inside.

You can view and purchse my work here, and keep upto date with my currents projects on my Instagram.
